User adoption is key to getting the most value from passbolt. Helping employees understand why passbolt
is being introduced and how it benefits their daily work can make onboarding smoother and encourage
long-term engagement.

To make this easier, passbolt provides ready-made onboarding materials that you can share with your
users before or during deployment.

## Available resources

The following resources are available to help you introduce passbolt to your organisation:

- **End user PDF presentation**: a ready-to-share guide that introduces users to passbolt and its key
  features. [Download the PDF](pathname:///files/passbolt-user-quick-start-guide.pdf).
- **Google Slides template**: a customisable version of the guide that you can adapt to your
  organisation's onboarding process. [Make a copy of the template](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/19KaC6KkkXTx9s4CLj_gZ7XLrN2-dqxfTuxRN3fvRxFw/copy).
- **Product tour video**: [Discover Passbolt: the open source password and secret management for teams](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=97JCXSj_Mdw), a walkthrough available on YouTube.
- **User Guide**: the in-depth [user guide](/user/) for answering more complex or technical questions
  down the line.

## What the presentation covers

The presentation provides a high-level introduction to:

- What passbolt is.
- Why organisations use a password manager.
- Secure password sharing and collaboration.
- Access management and team workflows.
- Browser extensions and available clients.
- Passbolt's security model and open-source approach.
- Getting started as a new user.

Common customisations you may want to make:

- Removing slides that are not relevant to your deployment, for example the mobile apps.
- Adding company-specific security policies or instructions.
- Applying your organisation's branding and visual identity.
