:::danger
If you are changing your domain from **HTTP to HTTPS**, you will unlink all users' browser extensions. Before making this change, you must ensure that all users have a copy of their private key to [recover their account](/user/quickstart/browser/setup-on-another-machine/).

**Pro tip:** To download their recovery kit, users can follow this [dedicated guide](/user/basic-features/browser/download-recovery-kit/)
:::
