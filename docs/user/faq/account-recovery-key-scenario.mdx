# Scenario: I've lost my private key

## Description

This page is a step-by-step user guide on how to proceed in case of a private key loss.

## Steps

### Step 0: Help, I lost my private key

:::info[Step.0]
When trying to log in, [on another machine for example](/user/quickstart/browser/setup-on-another-machine/), I notice that I do not have my private key. As the above picture is showing, I can click on "Help, I lost my private key".
:::

<Figure
  src="/img/help/2025/12/account-recovery-scenario-key-step0.png"
  alt="Help, I lost my private key"
  caption="fig. Help, I lost my private key"
/>

### Step 1: Request account recovery

:::info[Step.1]
As I lost my private key, I can click on the button "Request account recovery".
:::

<Figure
  src="/img/help/2025/12/account-recovery-scenario-key-step1.png"
  alt="Request account recovery"
  caption="fig. Request account recovery"
/>

### Step 2: Choose a new passphrase

:::info[Step.2]
The private key is linked with the passphrase. Thus, I need to choose a new passphrase and click on "Next".
:::

<Figure
  src="/img/help/2025/12/account-recovery-scenario-key-step2.png"
  alt="Choose a new passphrase"
  caption="fig. Choose a new passphrase"
/>

### Step 3: Customise the security token

:::info[Step.3]
I'm choosing here three letters and a colour to be able to quickly verify that the form is coming from passbolt, and click on "Next".
:::

<Figure
  src="/img/help/2025/12/account-recovery-scenario-key-step3.png"
  alt="Customise the security token"
  caption="fig. Customise the security token"
/>

### Step 4: Please wait, while your request is processed

:::info[Step.4]
When I'm ending up on this screen, I know that an email has been sent to administrators in order to accept my account recovery request. I need to wait until the approval which will come by email.
:::

<Figure
  src="/img/help/2025/12/account-recovery-scenario-key-step4-0.png"
  alt="Please wait, while your request is processed"
  caption="fig. Please wait, while your request is processed"
/>

:::info[Step.4]
Receiving this email, I can click on "Complete account recovery".
:::

:::caution
This link must be opened in the same web browser the passphrase was reset.
:::

<Figure
  src="/img/help/2025/12/account-recovery-scenario-key-step4-1.png"
  alt="Complete account recovery"
  caption="fig. Complete account recovery"
/>

### Step 5: Complete recovery

:::info[Step.5]
Here, I need to enter the new passphrase I chose, while checking the security token customisation. When entered, I can click on "Complete recovery".
:::

<Figure
  src="/img/help/2025/12/account-recovery-scenario-key-step5.png"
  alt="Complete recovery"
  caption="fig. Complete recovery"
/>

### Step 6: Store your recovery kit in a safe place

:::info[Step.6]
Time for me to store my new recovery kit in a safe place, to tick the box "I safely store my recovery kit", and to click on "Next".
:::

<Figure
  src="/img/help/2025/12/account-recovery-scenario-key-step6.png"
  alt="Store your recovery kit in a safe place"
  caption="fig. Store your recovery kit in a safe place"
/>

## The end

At this stage, you should have recovered a full access to your passbolt account!  
Thank you for reading.
